Uplifting the underprivileged

What is a better service towards the nation than working towards helping the underprivileged section of the society gain access towards basic civic amenities, education and livelihood so that they can actively contribute to the economy. We at Krishtara Divya Lau Trust conscientiously work towards this goal.

Education, food and shelter

Good food, shelter, proper education and training are the basic tools to empower someone to make his place in this harsh world. Unfortunately, not everyone on this planet have access to these. We at Krishtara Divya Lau Trust work at providing exactly these 4 basics of life to those who need our help.

About Us

Established in 2016, Krishtara Divya Lau Trust began it's operations from sector-19, Noida. 6 years down the line, today we are operational in whole of Noida and Indore.

Our mission and vision is to empower the underprivileged by providing them with food, education and jobs. For this, we regularly hold free food distribution in slums and roadside, help enrol their children into government schools so that they can get access to free education and mid-day meals, and lastly we organize vocational training courses to assist the unemployed members of the society gain financial independence by finding the right jobs that match their skillset.

The team of volunteers and trust members is headed by Ms Krishna Sharma, who is also a renowned astrologer. Under the guidance of Ms Krishna, we are able to organise free food distribution 10 times a month, support the education of 17 children from Noida and 6 from Indore, and organize vocational training courses in computer basics, fashion designing and spoken english for over 30 students every weekend.

Our Vision

Today we have presence in small areas of Noida and Indore. Our vision is to expand our presence pan-India and be able to reach out to all those people whose lives can be transformed with our help. At present, 60 per cent of India’s population, or an estimated 812 million people, live below that poverty line. Hence, we have a massive task ahead of us to be able to connect with these 812 million people and empower them to become active and contributing members of the society.

About our founder

Krishna Sharma

Chairman & founder
Our trust was founded with the concerted efforts of eminent Astrologer Krishna Sharma. She has dedicated her life to studying Hindu scriptures, particularly about Astrology and spreading that knowledge among the masses, removing superstitions and misunderstandings about Hinduism.

She is a known face in Indian Telivision channels that stream shows on astrology and has frequently appeared on well known news channels like AajTak, News18, TV9 Bharatvarsh, etc where she has not only appeared in weekly astrology shows as the host but also participated in numerous debates on religion and current affairs. She has used these platforms to stand for the truth and bust myths and prejudices imparted by psuedo Gurus in the name of Hinduism. She also creates content related to Astrology on her Youtube Channel - Krishtara Divya Lau Channel


Deeply inspired from the ideals of Hinduism, she dedicates a large part of her time to uplifting the underprivileged sections of the society by empowering them with education and vocational skills to gain employment. She also organises workshops and classes in temples and community halls to educate the general public about Sanskrit, the mother of all languages and the Shastras.


Till now she has single-handedly raised hunds and used her personal finances to achieve this mission, but your cooperations can help her in this noble task.
